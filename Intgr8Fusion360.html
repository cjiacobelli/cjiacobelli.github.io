INTGR8 Technologies Delivery Vehicle Concept

May-Aug 2020

Background

By developing concepts for a semi-autonomous delivery vehicle for INTGR8 Technologies, I honed my proficiency in fusion 360, while also improving my design and creative thinking skills.

The Project

A couple different models were created but I will only show one of them. Due to confidentiality reasons (since this is property of INTGR8 Technologies), I will describe the design without revealing the entire concept of the vehicle. This vehicle is semi-autonomous, meaning there is no driver area. Instead, it has small windows around the top end of the car, where cameras are to be placed. This concept features side and front doors as well as a raising roof and floor. Moreover, there are tracks on the floor for the shelving units to move on, which feature a variety of different size containers based on PO box sizes.

I created everything in this design, no parts were taking from external resources. Please look at the annotated slideshow below to view the renderings.
